Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to AIH ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclosure, and safeguard your information when you use our backup service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

• Account information (email, name)
• Backup data (files, memories, configurations)
• Usage data (backup frequency, storage usage)
• Payment information (processed securely by Stripe)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

• Provide and maintain our backup services
• Process your payments
• Send you important updates about your account
• Improve our services
• Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Encryption

All data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption both in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. Your encryption keys are generated on your device and never transmitted to our servers. We cannot access or read your backup data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

• Service providers (hosting, payment processing)
• Legal authorities when required by law
• Affiliated companies

6. Data Retention

We retain your backup data according to your subscription plan. After account cancellation, data is retained for 30 days, after which it is permanently deleted.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

• Access your personal data
• Correct inaccurate data
• Request deletion of your data
• Export your backups
• Withdraw consent at any time

Email: privacy@aih.app
