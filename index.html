Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How secure is my data? +

All data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption both in transit and at rest. We never access or read your backup data. Your encryption keys are stored separately from your data.

How often can I backup? +

Free plan: Manual backups only. Pro plan: Hourly, daily, or custom schedules. Team plan: Real-time backups with unlimited frequency.

Can I restore to a different device? +

Yes! Backups are tied to your account, not your device. Restore to any computer where you have OpenClaw installed.

What happens to my backups if I cancel? +

Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all your backups before cancelling. After 30 days, backups are permanently deleted.

Does it work with all AI assistants? +

Currently optimized for OpenClaw. We're working on support for other AI assistant platforms. Contact us if you have specific requirements.

What's the maximum backup size? +

Free plan: 100MB per backup. Pro plan: 1GB per backup. Team plan: 10GB per backup. Most users are well under these limits.
